  • PS001 will be detailed below.

PS001-Problem

  • We like to share. We like to show.
  • Our first product will make sure you show what you want to show. share what you want to share.
  • Leveraging state of the art Ai (computer vision), cryptography and Apple latest on device compute capacity.
  • PS001 help you find and hide content you do not want to show. We automatically surface sensitive content, You choose what you is safe.

PS001-Solution

  • Defining what is your "safe camera roll" PS001 will help you find content you do not wish to show.
    • My safe camera roll "work".
    • My safe camera roll "family".
  • Different setup you define to avoid undesirable show of your content.
    • You might not want your co-worker to see your fitting room underwear catwalk.
    • You might not want anybody to see your NSFW(Not Safe For Work) content.
  • It is ok to have NSFW content, as long as you are in control.

PS001-Technologies

  • On device media classification. Turn on Airplane mode, it still work.
  • Continuous learning Ai. We built a system to continuously improve accuracy.
  • On device media encryption using State-of-the-art cryptography and standards
  • Zero-knowledge privacy: Our encryption system is designed this way so that we wouldn't be able to see your media/file content or account infos under any circumstances.
  • Zero-log policy: We strip your media from any metadata(personal information & geo-tag), piece of mind if you decide to share.
